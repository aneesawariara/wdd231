Gold Membership Benefits

  • All Silver and Bronze benefits
  • Premium spotlight placement on the chamber homepage (extended duration)
  • Featured article or interview in the chamber newsletter
  • Unlimited promotional posts on chamber social media
  • Free vendor booth at annual chamber expo
  • Priority access to sponsorship opportunities and speaking slots
  • Quarterly strategy sessions with chamber leadership
  • Custom analytics report on chamber engagement and visibility

Silver Membership Benefits

  • All Bronze benefits
  • Spotlight feature on the chamber homepage (rotating monthly)
  • Access to exclusive business development workshops
  • Discounted rates for chamber-hosted vendor booths and expos
  • One-on-one strategy session with chamber advisors per year
  • Priority registration for high-demand events

Bronze Membership Benefits

  • Access to monthly networking mixers and community events
  • Business listing in the chamber directory
  • Inclusion in quarterly chamber newsletter
  • Member badge for your website
  • Opportunity to post one promotional offer per quarter on chamber social media

Non-Profit Membership Benefits

  • Inclusion in community outreach and volunteer events
  • Listing in the chamber's nonprofit directory
  • Access to grant-writing and fundraising workshops
  • Promotion of one annual campaign or event via chamber newsletter
  • Volunteer recruitment support through chamber channels
